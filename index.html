The story of terrabarn.

The past

Terracotta was the material of choice for cooking in ancient India because it established our connection with the earth.

The present

Terra Barn has reinvented the use of terracotta with the art of jewellery making.We endeavour to re-establish our connection with Mother Earth through our handcrafted products that enhance beauty and wellbeing.

Our Products

The nature-inspired designs are chic, contemporary and traditional, custom-made jewellery. We use clay, water, fabric colours,cotton,silk and zari threads.

The Process

The jewellery is made entirely by hand, from shaping the clay, to designing, stringing and colouring.
